## Story Read

Art of Animation should feel like walking into sketchbooks and storyboards that became full-scale Disney and Pixar worlds. The key guest-loved read is immersive, larger-than-life character environments: Finding Nemo around The Big Blue Pool, Cars, The Lion King, and The Little Mermaid, with family-friendly energy everywhere.

The current recreation PDF adds the lived-in daily rhythm: Pixel Play Arcade inside Animation Hall, Poolside Activities and Glow Party at The Big Blue Pool Deck, Play Disney Parks gallery activity, Find a Friend throughout the resort, Story Time Yoga and Movies Under the Stars on the movie lawn near The Big Blue Pool, and Campfire at Pride Rock between the Lion King buildings.

## Icon Direction

- Lead with an animation-paper/logo shape so the resort reads as art and story first.
- Put a bright brush stroke across the paper for the animation process.
- Use Big Blue Pool water as the bottom anchor.
- Represent the four story worlds through small colorful forms: Nemo/water, Cars-orange curve, Lion King green Pride Rock/lawn cue, and Little Mermaid magenta shell/fin.
- Add a tiny Animation Hall/Pixel Play pencil or animator mark.
- Add a small Skyliner gondola as a movement cue.

## Motion Notes

- Animation paper tilts slightly on hover/focus.
- Big Blue Pool water drifts down.
- Story-world pieces lift together.
- Skyliner slides a few pixels.
- Sparkles scale gently.
