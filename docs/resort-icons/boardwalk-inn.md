## Story Read

BoardWalk Inn should feel like turn-of-the-century Atlantic City brought into the EPCOT resort area: an elegant waterfront hotel wrapped in boardwalk nightlife, carnival energy, Crescent Lake paths, Surrey bikes, and Luna Park Pool.

Disney's BoardWalk Inn pool page anchors the resort in Luna Park Pool and the "elegance of turn-of-the-century Atlantic City." The shared BoardWalk pool detail page fills in the most distinctive visual language for the same Luna Park area: carnival-themed pool, 200-foot-long Keister Coaster waterslide, a 1920s boardwalk amusement-park feel, grinning elephant water-spout statues, and vintage-looking show posters.

The user-loved hook is that BoardWalk Inn feels alive after dark without losing its polished resort identity: lights, lake reflections, music, strollable waterfront energy, and an old seaside amusement mood right outside the room.

## Icon Direction

- Tall teal-roof BoardWalk facade for the inn identity.
- Warm striped marquee and light bulbs for boardwalk/carnival nightlife.
- Curving Keister Coaster-inspired waterslide arc over the water.
- Crescent Lake water and wood boardwalk planks at the base.
- Small lamp and elephant-fountain cue to hint at Luna Park Pool details without overcrowding the tiny card icon.

## Motion Notes

- Boardwalk planks settle down slightly on hover.
- Marquee rises a touch, like the lights coming alive.
- Coaster/waterslide glides forward.
- Elephant water cue shifts subtly.
- Sparkles rotate gently; reduced-motion disables transitions.
