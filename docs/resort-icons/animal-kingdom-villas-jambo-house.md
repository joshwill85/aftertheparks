## Story Read

Jambo House Villas should feel like a home base inside the grand Jambo House lodge. It shares the Jambo recreation calendar with Disney's Animal Kingdom Lodge, but the resort-card icon needs its own read: villa comfort, balcony/window rhythm, and a quieter sense of returning home after exploring the lobby, savanna overlooks, Uzima Springs, and Jambo activities.

The current Jambo recreation PDF anchors guest-facing details that make this side feel alive: Jambo House Lobby Art Tour, String of Memories activities, Jammin' at Jambo House, campfire at Arusha Savanna Overlook, movies at Hakuna Matata Playground, and Uzima Springs Pool Deck activity moments.

## Icon Direction

- Jambo House roofline remains present, but the main read shifts to villa balcony windows.
- Savanna overlook rails wrap through the middle, so the resort feels connected to animal-viewing spaces.
- String of Memories beads provide the activity-specific Jambo cue and a warmer villa-family detail.
- African art heart/medallion keeps the lobby art-tour story visible without repeating the Lodge icon exactly.
- Uzima water sits at the bottom as the recreation anchor.
- Small animal-life silhouettes keep the savanna present but secondary to the villa-home story.
