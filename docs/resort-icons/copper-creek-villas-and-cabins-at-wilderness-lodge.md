## Story Read

Copper Creek Villas and Cabins should feel like the modern rustic waterfront side of Wilderness Lodge: a Pacific Northwest-inspired base camp with villas and cabins, hidden trails, railway-past details, and the feeling of arriving at a private cabin porch beside Bay Lake. Its strongest recreation anchor is Copper Creek Springs Pool, where Disney describes a bubbling creek, a heated pool surrounded by nature, a rock-built 67-foot waterslide, whirlpool spas, and nearby Geyser Point.

The user-loved hook is the cabin retreat: warm windows, a porch rail, water at the edge, pine silhouettes, and the sense that the creek, pool, lake, and lodge glow all meet in one compact place.

## Icon Direction

- Waterfront cabin facade with warm window and wraparound porch rail.
- Bay Lake base with Copper Creek stream flowing into the pool.
- Rock waterslide and whirlpool ring for Copper Creek Springs Pool.
- Pine trail silhouettes to keep the hidden-trail/nature context.
- Railway-past cue through subtle track/tie geometry.
- Geyser Point glow as a small warm vertical accent near the water.
