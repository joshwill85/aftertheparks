## Story Read

All-Star Movies should feel like walking into a bright, larger-than-life Disney film set. The strongest official recreation cue is the pool source: guests are immersed in Disney storytelling at the Fantasia Pool and Duck Pond Pool, with the Fantasia Pool inspired by Fantasia and home to a Sorcerer Mickey fountain. The Duck Pond Pool is based on The Mighty Ducks film series and designed to resemble a hockey rink.

The guest-loved hook is recognizability and scale: families know the movie worlds quickly, Cinema Hall and Reel Fun Arcade reinforce the film-lot language, and Movie Under the Stars makes the resort feel like a playful movie-night campus instead of just themed lodging.

## Icon Direction

- Film reel and clapperboard as the primary resort identity cue.
- Sorcerer hat, star, and fountain stream for Fantasia Pool and Sorcerer Mickey.
- Turquoise pool water to keep the icon tied to recreation instead of only cinema.
- Hockey-rink sweep and puck for the Duck Pond Pool and Mighty Ducks detail.
- Marquee bulbs and sparkles for the movie-night, larger-than-life resort mood.

## Motion Notes

- Film reel tilts slightly on hover/focus.
- Sorcerer hat lifts with a tiny magical lean.
- Pool water shifts subtly.
- Hockey-rink detail slides just enough to feel active without distracting from the card.
