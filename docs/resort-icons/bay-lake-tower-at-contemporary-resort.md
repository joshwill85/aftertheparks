Disney's pool detail page anchors Bay Lake Tower around Bay Cove Pool,
overlooking Bay Lake, its 148-foot-long waterslide, zero-depth entry, bubbling
geyser water play area, poolside bar, and adjacency to the nearby Contemporary
Resort pools.

## Guest-Loved Theme Read

Bay Lake Tower should not reuse the Contemporary monorail story. Its emotional
hook is villa-style calm with a modern tower silhouette: balconies and water
views, direct connection to the Contemporary campus, and the feeling of having a
more tucked-away home base beside Bay Lake.

## Icon Direction

The icon uses five resort-specific symbols:

- Curved modern villa tower for the Bay Lake Tower silhouette.
- A small Sky Way Bridge gesture to suggest the Contemporary connection.
- Balcony rows for the view-focused villa feel.
- Bay Cove Pool shape with slide/geyser energy.
- Bay Lake water at the base.

The card version shares the same gentle motion family as the Contemporary icon:
water shifts, the bridge/spark subtly lifts, and pool bubbles feel alive. Reduced
motion users receive the static icon.
