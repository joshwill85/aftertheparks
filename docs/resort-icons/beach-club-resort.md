## Story Read

Beach Club should read as casual New England seaside resort energy, with Crescent Lake as the setting and Stormalong Bay as the unmistakable activity anchor. Disney's pool page makes Stormalong Bay the strongest visual source: a 3-acre water wonderland with a life-sized shipwreck replica, a 230-foot waterslide from the mast, an expansive sand-bottomed pool, lazy-river water, whirlpool spas, and a Crescent Lake-facing Tidal pool.

The user-loved hook is not just "pool" or "beach." It is the feeling of a lakeside beach club where the resort, water, shipwreck, bridges, and playful pool complex all collapse into one place-based memory.

## Icon Direction

- New England seaside roofline in cream and sea-blue.
- Shipwreck mast and sail in front, with the waterslide dropping into Stormalong Bay.
- Sand-bottom pool crescent and Crescent Lake water at the base.
- Small white bridge detail to nod to the Crescent Lake bridge/island experience.
- Warm sparkle accents for a polished magical resort-card feeling.

## Motion Notes

- Pool and water subtly lift on hover.
- Shipwreck mast rocks slightly.
- Slide shifts down and across.
- Roofline lifts by one pixel so the card has life without visual noise.
