## Story Read

All-Star Music should feel like a bright outdoor stage full of oversized music icons. The official pool source gives the clearest recreation story: the Calypso Pool is guitar-shaped and centered around a colorful Three Caballeros fountain, while bongos and maracas line the area. The Piano Pool adds a second unmistakable shape with black-and-white keyboard keys.

The guest-loved hook is that the resort is instantly legible and playful. Melody Hall, Note'able Games, music-themed neighborhoods, poolside activity energy, and movies at the Calypso Pool make the resort feel like a family-friendly music campus rather than a generic value resort.

## Icon Direction

- Large music note as the first-read resort theme.
- Guitar-shaped Calypso Pool as the main recreation cue.
- Three colorful fountain plumes for the Three Caballeros fountain without overloading the tiny icon.
- Piano keys for the Piano Pool.
- Maraca and bongo-like rhythm details to echo the official bongos and maracas language.
- Warm movie-night sparkle to connect the Calypso Pool evening screenings.

## Motion Notes

- Music note lifts and tilts slightly on hover/focus.
- Piano keys slide subtly.
- Guitar pool rocks gently, like a stage instrument.
- Fountain cue lifts while the water arcs continue the existing falls motion.
- Maracas/rhythm details rotate slightly.
