# The Cabins at Disney's Fort Wilderness Resort Icon

Figma component: https://www.figma.com/design/S8XwSQh6krHV3LrecKw3nF?node-id=30-42

## Story Anchors

- Rustic American Frontier cabin base camp, distinct from the broader Campsites identity.
- Pine and cypress trails, lush meadows, and the outdoor Fort Wilderness setting.
- Reception Outpost golf cart movement as a major guest-recognizable way to explore the resort.
- Meadow Swimmin' Pool and Wilderness Swimmin' Pool water, with a small slide/fort-play cue.
- Campground Theater campfire and movie glow for Chip 'n' Dale's Campfire Sing-A-Long and Movies Under the Stars.
- Bike Barn adventure cue for rentals and Wilderness Back Trail Adventure.
- Small Tri-Circle-D horseshoe cue, intentionally secondary so the icon does not become a horse-ranch icon.

## Design Notes

The icon is a warm cabin-first composition with a broad roof and porch glow, a moving golf cart, teal pool water, Campground Theater screen and campfire, pine/cypress silhouettes, and a small horseshoe. The hover animation keeps the story subtle: the golf cart glides, the pool shifts, the fire lifts, the cabin rises slightly, and the spark/horseshoe add a light magical accent.
