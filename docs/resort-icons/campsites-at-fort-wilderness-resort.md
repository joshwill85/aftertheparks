# The Campsites at Disney's Fort Wilderness Resort Icon

Figma component: https://www.figma.com/design/S8XwSQh6krHV3LrecKw3nF?node-id=33-49

## Story Anchors

- Backcountry campground identity first: tent and RV campsite loops, not cabins.
- Pine and cypress groves, lush meadows, and Floridian wilderness trails.
- Meadow Recreation Area and Bike Barn movement through rentals, volleyball, tennis, canoe/kayak, and trail activity.
- Chip 'n' Dale's Campfire Sing-A-Long plus outdoor movie glow as the signature nightly family ritual.
- Tri-Circle-D Ranch, Pioneer Hall wagon rides, pony rides, and trail riding as supporting frontier story cues.
- Electrical Water Pageant shoreline sparkle as the Campsites' extra evening magic along Bay Lake.

## Design Notes

This icon is intentionally more campground than cabin: a tent and RV carry the first read, while the campfire/movie screen, small wagon, pine silhouettes, shoreline water, and pageant sparkle tell the supporting Fort Wilderness story. The hover animation gives the RV a small glide, lifts the tent and campfire, ripples the water, and nudges the wagon without making the card feel busy.
