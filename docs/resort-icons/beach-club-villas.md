## Story Read

Beach Club Villas should feel like the quieter Disney Vacation Club side of the Beach/Yacht Club story: still New England beach-hotel elegance, still connected to Stormalong Bay, but with a more tucked-away home-base identity.

Disney's pool detail page gives the shared Stormalong Bay anchors: 3 acres of aquatic fun, a life-sized shipwreck replica, a 230-foot waterslide, sand-bottom pool, lazy river, whirlpool spas, and Shipwreck Pool. The villa-specific differentiator is the leisure-pool language: Dunes Cove pool is privately located at Disney's Beach Club Villas, and all resort guests can enjoy it.

The user-loved hook is the combination of "I can have Stormalong Bay nearby" and "I can retreat back to a calmer villa-side pool and home-like balcony setting."

## Icon Direction

- Villa dormer and balcony lines to make the icon read as Beach Club Villas, not just Beach Club Resort.
- Dunes Cove pool as the main water shape.
- Smaller shipwreck mast and slide as a shared Stormalong Bay echo.
- Crescent Lake water and a white bridge to keep the EPCOT resort-area setting.
- Warm sparkle accents for the magical card feeling.

## Motion Notes

- Water and pool shift subtly on hover.
- Small shared shipwreck mast rocks slightly.
- Slide glides by a few pixels.
- Villa balconies lift/fade gently for a home-base shimmer.
