# Disney's Saratoga Springs Resort & Spa Icon

Figma component: https://www.figma.com/design/S8XwSQh6krHV3LrecKw3nF?node-id=46-114

## Story Anchors

- The first read is late-1800s Saratoga Springs: Victorian spa-resort architecture, warm rooflines, porch/clubhouse energy, and a refined horse-racing retreat mood.
- The horse and winner-circle cue carry the resort's racing identity without turning the mark into a generic horse icon.
- Spring water and the Lake Buena Vista/Disney Springs waterfront sit at the base, connecting the resort's spa story to its real water setting.
- Golf green and island-green color cues represent Disney's Lake Buena Vista Golf Course and the manicured greens woven around the resort.
- The Paddock Pool water tower and slide are included as a resort-specific guest-memory landmark.
- High Rock Spring Pool is represented through spring-water and slide language, with Donald Duck aquatic play kept as a small family-pool cue rather than a full character illustration.
- Community Hall and Horsing Around Rentals are shown through the small clubhouse/window block, tying together crafts, equipment checkout, bikes, surrey bikes, tennis, and resort-day activity flow.

## Design Notes

The icon should feel polished, equestrian, and Victorian rather than rustic, beachy, or purely golf-themed. The resort building and horse/winner-circle cue give the theme; the spring-water base, green fairway, pool-slide/water-tower cue, and Community Hall/Horsing Around element make it unmistakably Saratoga Springs. Hover motion stays light: water glides, the Victorian roofline lifts, the green settles, foliage sways, the Paddock slide nudges, the spring glints, and the horse cue moves just enough to feel alive.
