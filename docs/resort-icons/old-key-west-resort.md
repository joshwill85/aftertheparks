# Disney's Old Key West Resort Icon

Figma component: https://www.figma.com/design/S8XwSQh6krHV3LrecKw3nF?node-id=34-55

## Story Anchors

- Conch Flats and Florida Keys village energy first: pastel villa porch, warm color, palms, and water.
- Sandcastle Pool as the signature guest memory, including the giant sandcastle, 125-foot waterslide, dolphin fountain, and sand-beach feel.
- Lighthouse sauna by the pool deck as the tall recognizable silhouette.
- Shimmering canal and Lake Buena Vista Golf Course fairway path as the quiet Old Key West movement cue.
- Surrey bikes and Hank's Rent 'N Return as the activity cue without letting rentals dominate the icon.
- Electric Eel Gameroom appears only as a small spark so the icon still reads like the resort, not an arcade.

## Design Notes

The icon is built around a first-read Conch Flats villa and Sandcastle Pool, then layers the lighthouse, palms, canal/fairway, surrey bike, dolphin fountain, and Electric Eel spark as supporting story details. The hover motion keeps it easy on the card: water ripples, the surrey bike glides, the palms sway, and the lighthouse/villa lift slightly.
