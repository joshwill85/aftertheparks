## Story Read

Caribbean Beach Resort should feel like a bright island escape around Barefoot Bay: colorful villages, beaches, hammocks, tropical paths, Old Port Royale energy, Disney Skyliner access, and the more theatrical Fuentes del Morro Pool.

Disney's pool detail page gives the strongest icon anchor. Fuentes del Morro Pool is described as a zero-entry pirate's paradise themed like a colonial Spanish fortress, with 2 waterslides, man-of-war cannon spray, a shipwreck play area, mini slides, a crow's nest, and a drenching barrel. The official recreation index also frames the pool image as a Spanish Colonial themed swimming pool.

The user-loved hook is that Caribbean Beach has two rhythms at once: a relaxed island lagoon stay with beaches and hammocks, and a playful pirate-fort pool world that makes the resort feel especially alive for families. The Skyliner adds the modern movement cue that many guests associate with staying there.

## Icon Direction

- Spanish-fortress pool tower as the primary silhouette, instead of a generic tropical icon.
- Barefoot Bay water and sandy beach as the foundation.
- Twin waterslide arc, cannon splash, and bright pool accents for Fuentes del Morro.
- Palm and hammock for the laid-back island village feeling.
- Small Skyliner gondola crossing the sky to anchor the resort's transportation identity.
- Warm tropical sparkles to keep the icon magical without losing the official story cues.

## Motion Notes

- Skyliner gondola glides slightly on hover.
- Water and beach layers settle gently.
- Fortress rises just a touch.
- Palm and hammock move subtly for island-breeze energy.
- Cannon splash shifts forward; reduced-motion disables transitions.
