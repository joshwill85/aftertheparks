## Story Read

The Villas share the Grand Floridian's Victorian elegance, Seven Seas Lagoon setting, monorail access, and Beach Pool recreation story, but the emotional center is different: this is the home-base version of the resort. The official resort page emphasizes Victorian-style splendor with the comforts of home, while the recreation sources add lagoon views, Beach Pool energy, the walking path, and fireworks.

The guest-loved hook is that the Villas feel calmer and more residential while still being steps from the resort's big magic: lagoon water, monorail movement, pool days, fireworks, and the Wedding Pavilion view.

## Icon Direction

- Victorian-style villa facade, roofline, and balcony as the primary silhouette.
- Softer home-base cue: balcony rail, windows, and compact villa shape.
- Seven Seas Lagoon water and quiet pool cove at the base.
- Monorail and walking-path line as a practical Magic Kingdom-area cue.
- Small Wedding Pavilion, palm, and fireworks details to distinguish it from the main Grand Floridian icon.
