## Story Read

Wilderness Lodge should read as the main National Park lodge story: Pacific Northwest craft, pine forest, mountains, a bear badge, a massive timber lodge, and the grand lobby fireplace. The current activity sheet reinforces the core guest experience around the Resort Lobby Fireplace, Wonders of the Lodge Art & Architecture Tour, nature walk, Humphrey's Hideout, Copper Creek Springs Pool, Geyser Point, campfire, and Reunion Station lawn movies.

The user-loved hook is that the resort feels like a complete wilderness retreat beside Bay Lake: rugged and warm at once. Guests remember the soaring lodge, the fire, the trees, the water, the geyser, and the sense that the whole place has a mythic handcrafted story.

## Icon Direction

- National Park badge energy with mountains, pine trees, and a bear.
- Timber lodge facade with a glowing hearth at the center.
- Copper Creek Springs Pool and rock waterslide.
- Bay Lake/Copper Creek water base.
- Geyser spray and campfire/pageant sparkle for nighttime magic.
