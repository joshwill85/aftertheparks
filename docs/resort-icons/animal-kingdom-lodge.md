## Story Read

Animal Kingdom Lodge should read as a living savanna lodge first. The official pool page anchors the recreation side with Uzima Springs Pool facing Uzima Savanna, surrounded by canopy trees and palms, with a natural-beach zero-depth entry and waterslide. The current Jambo recreation PDF adds guest-facing Lodge rituals: Jambo House Lobby Art Tour, String of Memories activities, animal tracking at Arusha Savanna Overlook, campfire at Arusha Savanna Overlook, Hakuna Matata Playground movies, and Simba's Activity Center.

The guest-loved hook is immersion: stepping from the warm Jambo House lobby into animal overlooks where the resort feels alive at every hour. The icon should feel like a lodge opening toward a savanna, not a generic animal badge.

## Icon Direction

- Warm sunrise/sunset circle to carry the lobby and savanna glow.
- Kraal-inspired lodge roofline as the main architectural read.
- Savanna tree and animal silhouettes for the living preserve.
- Giraffe and zebra cues to make the animal-view experience immediate without copying official character art.
- Uzima water and overlook strokes at the bottom to tie the resort to recreation.
- African art medallion in the foreground to honor the Jambo House art-tour and collection story.

## Motion Notes

- Savanna tree leans subtly on hover/focus.
- Lodge roof lifts a single pixel so the building feels warm but stable.
- Animal cues shift slightly as a quiet living-savanna signal.
- Medallion rises and scales gently to bring the African art layer forward.
- Water/overlook strokes use the shared water drift.
