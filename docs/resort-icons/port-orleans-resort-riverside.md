# Disney's Port Orleans Resort - Riverside Icon

Figma component: https://www.figma.com/design/S8XwSQh6krHV3LrecKw3nF?node-id=43-102

## Story Anchors

- Rural Louisiana is the first-read mood: warm river light, cypress silhouettes, wooden bridgework, and the Sassagoula River cutting through the resort.
- The two resort neighborhoods are both present: Magnolia Bend appears as a stately mansion cue, while Alligator Bayou appears through cypress and wooded island language.
- Ol' Man Island is the center of the mark, with the abandoned sawmill/swimmin' hole/fishin' hole story carrying the resort-specific identity.
- The 95-foot rustic waterslide is drawn as a millworks slide curling into the water.
- The three wooden bridges are abstracted into the bridge rail across the foreground.
- Cane-pole fishing and Fishin' Hole are shown with a small pole, line, and float.
- Riverside Levee Marina and the carriage rides are represented through a tiny carriage, with surrey-bike movement kept as a supporting source-backed cue.

## Design Notes

The icon should feel calm, wooded, and storybook-rustic rather than Mardi Gras-bright. The sawmill and bridge give the first read; the mansion/cypress pairing keeps Magnolia Bend and Alligator Bayou both present; the river, fishing pole, carriage, and waterslide add the guest-loved details. Hover motion stays small: the Sassagoula ripples, cypress sways, bridge settles, fishing line lifts, and the carriage rolls forward.
