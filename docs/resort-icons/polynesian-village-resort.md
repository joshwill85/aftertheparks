# Disney's Polynesian Village Resort Icon

Figma component: https://www.figma.com/design/S8XwSQh6krHV3LrecKw3nF?node-id=35-57

## Story Anchors

- South Pacific arrival fantasy first: warm tropical sunset, palms, tiki torch, lei, and Great Ceremonial House roofline.
- Lava Pool as the signature resort memory, including the towering volcano, waterfall feeling, and 142 foot-long waterslide.
- Seven Seas Lagoon as the resort's emotional edge: beach, marina, jogging path, Magic Kingdom views, and evening shoreline watching.
- Monorail glide as a small but important identity cue for this Magic Kingdom-area resort.
- Moana's Voyage and canoe/wayfinding energy as the modern family-facing story layer.
- Electrical Water Pageant sparkle as the nighttime lagoon magic.
- Beach volleyball and Seven Seas Marina remain supporting source cues, not first-read symbols.

## Design Notes

The icon is intentionally an arrival scene, not a resort activity collage. The Great Ceremonial House, palms, tiki torch, and lei tell the welcome story first; the Lava Pool volcano and slide carry the strongest recreation identity; the Moana canoe, monorail, lagoon, marina/beach, and pageant lights add the resort-specific magic without overcrowding the card. Hover motion keeps it gentle: the lagoon ripples, monorail glides, canoe moves, palms sway, and pageant sparkle brightens.
