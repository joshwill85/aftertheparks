# Disney's Riviera Resort Icon

Figma component: https://www.figma.com/design/S8XwSQh6krHV3LrecKw3nF?node-id=45-110

## Story Anchors

- The first read is Europe imagined by Disney: a sunlit Mediterranean/Riviera tower, warm stucco, arched resort architecture, gardens, and waterfront color.
- The mosaic panel carries the resort's art identity and guest-loved walkway/mosaic memory, with an art-ribbon cue for the Eventi Room, Art de la Mosaique, and Painting on the Riviera activity energy.
- The Disney Skyliner gondola is shown overhead because arrival by Skyliner is one of the resort's defining, useful guest experiences.
- The Riviera Pool is represented by the pool water and the stately column-tower slide.
- S'il Vous Play is represented as a small Fantasia fountain/splash cue, with the more relaxed Beau Soleil mood kept in the calmer water and garden color.
- The lower waterfront reads as resort-day leisure: Riviera pool deck, waterfront campfire, Movie Lawn, and nature-walk/Barefoot Bay cues without overloading the tiny image.

## Design Notes

The icon should feel refined, sunny, artful, and transportive rather than nautical, carnival, or beach-club themed. The tower and mosaic give the resort's elegant identity; the Skyliner, pool slide, Fantasia fountain, gardens, and water give it guest-memory specificity. Hover motion stays light: the water glides, the tower lifts, the mosaic catches light, the gardens sway, the Skyliner drifts, and the fountain/spark cues brighten.
