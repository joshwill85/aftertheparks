# Disney's Pop Century Resort Icon

Figma component: https://www.figma.com/design/S8XwSQh6krHV3LrecKw3nF?node-id=38-55

## Story Anchors

- Walk-through-the-decades nostalgia first: 1950s, 1960s, 1970s, 1980s, and 1990s pop culture in one bright value-resort postcard.
- Hippy Dippy Pool as the signature emotional center, with a flower-shaped water-jet cue and 1960s energy.
- Bowling Pool and Computer Pool as supporting decade icons, including the bowling-pin shape and computer/floppy-disk language.
- Rubik-style cube and yo-yo as compact larger-than-life courtyard-object cues.
- Hourglass Lake and the Generation Gap bridge as the resort's walking-path and Art of Animation connection.
- Disney Skyliner as the modern movement cue that many guests associate with staying here.
- Everything POP and Fast Forward Arcade remain small energy cues, not the first-read symbol.

## Design Notes

The icon is meant to feel like a pop-culture memory collage, but still read cleanly at card size. The Hippy Dippy flower pool is the center, with the bowling pin, computer/floppy screen, Rubik-style cube, yo-yo, Hourglass Lake/Generation Gap rail, and Skyliner arranged as supporting story cues. Hover motion is playful but small: water ripples, the flower turns, the Skyliner glides, and the decades objects bounce gently.
