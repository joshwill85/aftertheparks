## Story Read

Boulder Ridge Villas should feel related to Wilderness Lodge, but quieter and more residential. Disney frames the resort around 1860s railroad hotels of the American West, rustic elegance, and the comforts of home. The pool source gives Boulder Ridge a specific visual anchor: Boulder Ridge Cove Pool, a laid-back oasis with zero-depth entry, a whirlpool spa, boulders, and shaded seating.

The user-loved hook is the tucked-away villa feeling: still wrapped in pine, stone, timber, and lodge warmth, but more like a hidden cove and home base than the grand main lodge moment.

## Icon Direction

- Rustic villa gable and porch as the home-base centerpiece.
- Boulder Ridge Cove Pool with turquoise water, zero-depth shimmer, and whirlpool ring.
- Rounded boulders and pine shade around the water.
- Railroad-hotel cue through track/tie geometry under the villa.
- Small lantern/hearth glow to preserve the Wilderness Lodge family connection.
