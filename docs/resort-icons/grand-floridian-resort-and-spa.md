## Story Read

Grand Floridian should feel elegant, bright, and waterside: ornate Victorian architecture, Seven Seas Lagoon, palms, the resort monorail, and the playful Beach Pool setting. The official pool source calls out the Beach Pool overlooking Seven Seas Lagoon, a walking bridge with a picturesque view, cascading waterfalls, a waterslide, and beach-like zero-depth entry.

The guest-loved hook is the resort's contrast: refined Victorian arrival energy with practical vacation magic close by, including lagoon views, monorail movement, fireworks, and poolside details that make the resort feel alive instead of formal.

## Icon Direction

- Victorian roofline and turret as the primary silhouette.
- Seven Seas Lagoon water and warm beach/pool shapes at the base.
- Monorail beam and train as a quick location cue.
- Beach Pool bridge, cascading waterfall, and waterslide as the user-loved recreation detail.
- Palms and small fireworks sparkles to keep the icon magical without turning it into a generic castle-like mark.

## Motion Notes

- Monorail glides horizontally on hover/focus.
- Lagoon and pool forms shift subtly.
- Turret lifts slightly.
- Fireworks sparkle scales and rotates.
