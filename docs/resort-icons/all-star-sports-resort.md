## Story Read

All-Star Sports should feel energetic, sunny, and immediately athletic. The official pool detail page gives the strongest visual story: Surfboard Bay Pool is a beach-like swimming hole with giant three-story surfboards, while Grand Slam Pool is shaped like a baseball diamond with Goofy on a pitcher's mound lobbing water at swimmers. The same source calls out the outfield-like sundeck and huge three-story baseball bats.

The guest-loved hook is scale and play. The resort works because guests can instantly tell which sports world they are walking through: surfboards, bats, fields, courts, jogging, arcade energy, and poolside games all make it feel like a cheerful sports campus.

## Icon Direction

- Giant surfboards as the first-read shape for Surfboard Bay.
- Blue-green pool water to keep the icon tied to recreation.
- Baseball diamond and ball for Grand Slam Pool.
- Goofy pitcher-fountain cue as a stylized blue cap and water streams.
- Baseball bats and ball details around the edge to make the sports theme unmistakable.
- Warm sparkle for poolside activities and game-day energy.

## Motion Notes

- Surfboards lift and lean slightly on hover/focus.
- Baseball diamond settles and scales gently.
- Goofy fountain cue shifts upward while the water uses existing falls motion.
- Baseball bats rotate subtly.
