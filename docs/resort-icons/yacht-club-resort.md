## Story Read

Yacht Club should feel more formal and nautical than Beach Club. The shared activity calendar and pool details connect it to Stormalong Bay, Shipwreck Beach, Crescent Lake, Bayside Marina, Ship Shape Fitness Center, and the shared Yacht & Beach Club lighthouse mark. The Yacht Club-specific read should lead with a polished New England yacht-club identity: navy trim, lighthouse/cupola, burgee flag, compass, anchor, marina water, and the tranquil Admiral Pool garden.

The user-loved hook is the feeling of staying at an elegant lakeside club that still gives immediate access to the huge Stormalong Bay world. It should not read as only a beach or only a pool; it should feel like a nautical home base beside Crescent Lake.

## Icon Direction

- Navy-and-white yacht-club facade with lighthouse/cupola and red burgee.
- Compass and anchor crest to make the nautical-club read immediate at small size.
- Crescent Lake marina water across the base.
- Admiral Pool garden calm as a quieter Yacht Club-specific pool cue.
- Small Shipwreck Beach/Stormalong Bay sail/mast cue so guests recognize the shared activity center without duplicating Beach Club's shipwreck-first icon.

## Motion Notes

- Lighthouse lifts subtly on hover.
- Burgee flag moves sideways like a small breeze.
- Compass rotates slightly.
- Admiral Pool and marina water shift gently.
- Small Shipwreck Beach cue rocks lightly.
