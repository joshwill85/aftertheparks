# Disney's Polynesian Villas & Bungalows Icon

Figma component: https://www.figma.com/design/S8XwSQh6krHV3LrecKw3nF?node-id=37-54

## Story Anchors

- Villa-home-on-the-lagoon feeling first: an overwater bungalow on stilts, warm sunset, dock posts, and Seven Seas Lagoon water.
- Island Tower as the newer home-base cue, kept secondary so the icon does not become a generic tower badge.
- Cove Pool and zero-entry water as the resort-specific recreation layer for Island Tower guests.
- Moana canoe and sail as a small wayfinding cue tied to Moana's Voyage and the Polynesian water story.
- Oasis Patio, Pineapple Lanai, and lounge/patio details as supporting vacation-at-home signals.
- Seven Seas Lagoon as the aquatic red carpet to Magic Kingdom, with a tiny pageant sparkle for nighttime lagoon magic.
- Distinct from Disney's Polynesian Village Resort: no Great Ceremonial House or monorail as the lead image.

## Design Notes

The icon is deliberately quieter and more residential than the Polynesian Village mark. The bungalow is the hero, the Island Tower and Cove Pool sit behind it, and the Moana canoe, patio/pineapple detail, palm, dock, and lagoon sparkle add the small magical cues. Hover motion keeps the card alive without making it busy: lagoon and cove ripple, bungalow and tower lift, canoe drifts, patio detail lifts, palm sways, and pageant sparkle brightens.
