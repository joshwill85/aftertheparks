## Story Read

Kidani Village should feel like the quieter savanna village-home side of Animal Kingdom Villas. The icon must not read as another Jambo House. Jambo gets the grand lodge and villa balcony rhythm; Kidani gets a lower village arch, Pembe Savanna Overlook evenings, Samawati Springs and Uwanja Camp water-play energy, Community Hall games and equipment checkout, and a wellness cue from Survival of the Fittest.

The current Kidani PDF anchors the lived-in resort rhythm: Find a Friend throughout Kidani Village, Survival of the Fittest open 24 hours, String of Memories activities at Palace Library, Pembe Savanna Overlook, and Kidani Village Lobby, Community Hall from 10:00am-10:00pm, poolside activities at Samawati Springs Pool, campfire and movie nights at Pembe Savanna Overlook, and paid arts/crafts in Palace Library.

## Icon Direction

- Use a lower arched Kidani lodge/village form instead of the taller Jambo roofline.
- Put Pembe Savanna Overlook rails through the middle so the evening overlook is a clear cue.
- Use Samawati/Uwanja water at the bottom as the recreation anchor.
- Add a small Community Hall/play shape and sports/check-out cue near the water.
- Keep String of Memories beads as a shared Animal Kingdom Villas family marker, but place them lower and looser than Jambo.
- Add a small wellness arc for Survival of the Fittest so Kidani's 24-hour fitness detail is not lost.

## Motion Notes

- Village arch lifts subtly on hover/focus.
- Overlook rails drift one pixel with the water.
- Community Hall/play cue rises slightly.
- Beads move diagonally up to feel collected through the day.
- Palms and wellness cue rotate gently.
- Spark scales slightly, preserving a magical feel without continuous animation.
